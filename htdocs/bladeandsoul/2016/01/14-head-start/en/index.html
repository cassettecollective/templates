The moment you've all been waiting for is now just around the corner! As a Founder's Pack owner, you will have access to the Blade & Soul Head Start period and the items included in your Founder's Pack beginning January 15th at 10:00AM PST. Don't forget to redeem your Founder's Pack coupon once Head Start has begun to receive the items included in your pack.

Want to upgrade your Founder's Pack? Founder's Packs will no longer be available once Blade & Soul launches on January 18th at 9:00PM PST, but there is still some time left to take advantage of the benefits the packs have to offer. Aside from all of the benefits and premium membership you get from owning a Disciple or Master pack, this will be the only time to get the costumes and the weapon skin that are exclusive to the Master Founder's Pack.

To celebrate the official Launch of Blade & Soul on January 18th at 9:00PM PST, we're holding a Launch Event in Southern California at the Santa Ana eSports Arena on the evening of January 18th starting at 6:00PM. Come celebrate with us, or watch the event live at http://stream.bladeandsoul.com.
